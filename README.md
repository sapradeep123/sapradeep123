## Featured Enterprise Projects

### Tier0 — AI-Native Managed Services Platform
Enterprise platform engineered for AI-native managed services delivery. Architect of the multi-tenant orchestration layer, agentic workflow engine, and operational intelligence dashboard. Built on FastAPI, LangGraph, PostgreSQL, Redis, and Celery with Docker-native deployment.

> *Multi-agent orchestration · Operational intelligence · Enterprise automation · FastAPI · LangGraph · PostgreSQL · Docker*

---

### AlphhaCRM — Enterprise CRM with AI Agent Layer
Full-stack enterprise CRM platform with embedded AI agents for sales cycle management, customer intelligence, and automated workflow execution. Features a document management system with semantic search, real-time analytics, and role-based access control.

> *CRM architecture · AI agents · Document management · FastAPI · React · PostgreSQL · Docker*

---

### TicketAI — Intelligent IT Service Management Platform
Enterprise ITSM platform with AI-powered ticket triage, multi-channel ingestion (Telegram, Discord, Email), priority classification, and SLA management. Backend engineered with FastAPI and Celery; frontend with React.

> *ITSM · AI triage · Multi-channel integration · FastAPI · Celery · React · PostgreSQL*

---

### OCR Intelligence Platform — Enterprise Document Processing
Production-grade document intelligence platform with multi-format OCR, PDF-to-structured-data extraction, benchmark evaluation, and batch processing APIs. Designed for enterprise document workflows with accuracy tracing and audit logs.

> *OCR · Document intelligence · PDF processing · NLP · Python · FastAPI · Benchmarking*

---

### CyberInsure — AI-Augmented Cyber Insurance Platform
Enterprise platform for cyber risk assessment, policy management, and AI-driven underwriting workflows. Integrates threat intelligence pipelines and structured risk scoring models for insurance carriers.

> *Cyber risk · Insurance technology · AI underwriting · Risk scoring · FastAPI · TypeScript*

---

### LMSwithLabs — Enterprise Learning Management System
Full-stack LMS engineered with AI-powered assessment generation, lab environment orchestration, health analytics, and institutional reporting. Built with TypeScript, Prisma ORM, Docker lab containers, and sprint-based delivery.

> *EdTech · LMS · AI assessments · Lab orchestration · TypeScript · Prisma · Docker*
